About Us

In 2015, we started a heating and AC Company called Itzep HVAC with the purpose of serving our neighbors in Los Angeles CA area and surrounding communities. Our beliefs are that you should have access to high quiality, professional, and courteous Los Angeles air conditioning service at an affordable price. We began by offering air conditioning service calls, preventive maintenance checks, and replacement air conditioning units.

Itzep HVACC is a first generation, family owned and operated Los Angeles CA air conditioning and heating service company. We are dedicated to our clients. For us, working in the heating and air conditioning industry is more than just a job, it’s our way of life. Contact us and you will see our motivation – your comfort and satisfaction.

We service all brands of heating and air conditioning systems, and we specialize in all aspects of residential indoor climate control, meaning that we go beyond HVAC service. We strive to find the comfort level you desire in your business or home.
